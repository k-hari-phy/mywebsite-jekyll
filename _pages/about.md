I am a Post Doc at IIT Bombay. I work at the interface of quantum field theory, gravitation, and quantum information. My research explores how quantum effects manifest in curved spacetime and how they can be probed using low-energy experimental platforms.

My Research Interest
======
- Quantum Field Theory in curved spacetime
- Relativistic Quantum Information
- Quantum Optics and Interferometry
- Gravitational wave detection
- Low energy Quantum gravity experiments

My works can be viewed [here](https://inspirehep.net/authors/1813548?ui-citation-summary=true).
